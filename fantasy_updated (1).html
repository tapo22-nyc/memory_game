How this fantasy game will work

1. Select 11 players from both teams.

2. You get 120 coins for each match.

3. Player cost equals their auction price in Cr. Example: 18 Cr = 18 coins.

4. Choose 1 captain and 1 vice-captain.

5. Captain gets 2x points. Vice-captain gets 1.5x points.

6. Scores will be updated manually after the 1st innings and after the match ends.
