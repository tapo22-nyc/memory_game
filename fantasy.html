How this fantasy game will work

1. Select 11 players from both teams.

2. Choose 1 captain and 1 vice-captain.

3. Captain gets 2x points. Vice-captain gets 1.5x points.

4. Scores will be updated manually after the 1st innings and after the match ends.

5. Leaderboard will update after each score refresh.
